Atomic Housing Competition

We organize esports events and tournaments every month, so members can meet up to discuss our wonderful shared hobby and compete at the top of our game, in tiny houses! At our events, we provide everything from public seating, to games and activities for entertainment, with casual LANs happening constantly. All games are broadcasted live on Twitch, and we have public viewing and touring areas for Tiny House prospective buyers. Entry fees are 10 Euros and we supply refreshments. Come find us and sign up for an upcoming event near you!

Bronze League

The Bronze League (Levels 1-10) returns this July as the main event of the month at 1:00 pm in the Groninger Forum. The Bronze League prize pool will be 20 euros for 3rd place, 30 euros for 2nd place, and a grand prize of 75 euros for the 1st Place winner of the Bronze League. All participants will get at least 100 EXP (EXperience Points) for attending this event. Events will proceed in a Round Robin format, with the winner at the end of the night having the most total wins. People who aren't registered, and merely want to come take a look are free to participate in this more casual tournament as well.

Silver League

The Silver League (Levels 11-20) is back on the 21st of August in the Groninger Forum. Had a taste in the AvV league of real competition? Now you can hone your skills in an all day rotation of best of 8 brackets. You can compete in as many events as you want, allowing you to refine and practice to grind up to Gold League. This is your chance to prove yourself and earn a promotion to Gold League, with each win giving you 4000 EXP (EXperience Points). The prize pool for 3rd Place is 50 Euros, 2nd Place is 75 euros, and the 1st Place finish will get a grand prize of 100 euros!

Gold League

The highest level of competition you can get, right here. The Gold League (Levels 21+) starts this September with a BOOM! This event is a bracket for 32 competitors, competing over 3 days in Groningen, in a fierce battle of the highest ranked and most well known players in the scene. This event will include special bonus rounds, where you can get a chance to take on the event organizers, and even game developers themselves! Now's your chance to gain tons of bonus EXP by competing against veterans!

Amateur vs Veteran

This upcoming Saturday is our biggest event day! It's the bi-monthly Amateur League vs Veteran League. Each newcomer/amateur gets a chance to challenge a veteran in a heated competition. Are you a level 5 or below competitor? Come have a shot at gaining massive experience against a tournament veteran at the Groninger Forum!

Showcase

If you also want to showcase your work, then this event is a perfect fit for you! We have a Showcase Event this upcoming Sunday, where you can put your work on display to attract new players, and even developer insight!

Handicraft

Do you prefer making your own designs with your own hands, well then this event is 'handmade' just for you! Come on down, and use our publicly available materials to design your very own custom tiny house! You can even create your own order and blueprints for the house right then and there!

HOUSES & PRICES

The term ‘Tiny House’ is most commonly used to describe a compact dwelling that is transportable and therefore inherently flexible in the nature of how it can perform in a variety of housing situations. While people are interested in tiny houses for various reasons, most are drawn to the flexibility and freedom they offer. For some, it’s the freedom to live a well-balanced life by purchasing something small within their means rather than relying on a mortgage. For others, it’s the flexibility of being able to change the size, function and location of a home as the need arises.

ABOUT US

At Atomic Houses, we firmly believe that the interactive way to introduce Tiny Houses to the general public is through E-Sport Events. We are a small group of individuals from all around the world, and we're trying to give the world more space, one Tiny House at a time. We are 2 designers an artist and 2 developers. We are a multi-cultural development team that seeks to work together, drawing our different views into our projects, and infusing them into our approaches.
